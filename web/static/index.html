How the model works

A scikit-learn pipeline (imputation, one-hot encoding, scaling, and the best of three compared regressors — XGBoost wins) trained on ~1,500 listings priced at Indian metro-city rates. The 90% confidence range comes from conformalized quantile regression. Point-model R² is 0.91 on held-out test data.
